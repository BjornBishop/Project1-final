Near-Shore Success

Although many felt the joy of working from home during the pandemic, often times these days, companies are requesting people come back to the office to build more committed teams. Having said that, there are increasing opportunities of clients looking to reduce overheads by having consultants working from near-shore locations. These often involve freelancers working from home 99% of the time with occassional visists to the company country. When and where possible, we push clients to take advantage of the remote setup and give you the flexbility you so deserve.
